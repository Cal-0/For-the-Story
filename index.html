About Me

For The Story Productions LLC is a tiny freelance media firm started by me, a Florida State University graduate with a Master’s in Communication, residing in sunny Tallahassee, Florida. As an enthusiast for the art of storytelling through film lenses from a very young age, I’ve done my best to find the practices and conventions that the greatest storytellers in the film industry use to build impactful stories for the big screen. For many years, I’ve been fascinated with directors’ and cinematographers’ ability to utilize the camera to tell their stories uniquely to enhance their visual storytelling. And let’s not forget sound’s imperative role as well, as it elevates the storytelling by bringing the accompanying visual to life. I truly believe nothing helps human beings connect more than impactful storytelling in all forms. A big chunk of my professional journey is blessed with many beautiful experiences that have allowed me to hone my visual communications skills through hands-on production and design work. Throughout those experiences, I’ve identified many factors and practices that help deliver incredible media works. I believe they all lead back to the robust nature behind creative storytelling. All those passionate learning adventures led to For The Story’s current mission to dig deep and build a powerful outlet for those who need to tell their story through their medium preference, whether through film or a simple photo. The stories we tell of people immerse others into fantastical universes that encourage thought, have overwhelming sentiment, and maybe even provide much-needed inspiration. We believe the most powerful stories are the ones you tell ourselves… Let’s make them extraordinary for the story.
